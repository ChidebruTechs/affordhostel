Quick Access to Client's Fragmented Pages

This web app aims to help campus students find hostels from the comfort of their phones, eliminating the need to manually scout for diaspora accommodation as has been the norm.

The platform is expected to scale across all campuses nationwide. Initial launch will focus on Maseno University in August 2025, targeting the September intake.

Please use this page to navigate quickly through the rough sketches and early page layouts created by the client.

For the hostel view layout, consider modeling it after the card-style listings found on Boma Yangu.

Note:

  • This page was created for demonstration and navigation purposes.
  • The project was forked from the client’s GitHub; the client is not a developer but a DIY enthusiast.
  • In the homepage, you can use any number and random password to sign in — it's just a placeholder to give you insight into dropdowns and hostel categories.
  • A new branch will be created for development using React and Django.
